# Biography

Dr. Jason Bryer is currently an Assistant Professor and Associate Director in the [Data Science and Information Systems](https://sps.cuny.edu/academics/graduate/master-science-data-science-ms) department at the City University of New York. He is currently the Principal Investigator of the FIPSE ($3 million [#P116F150077](https://fipse.ed.gov/grantshow.cfm?grantNumber=P116F150077)) and IES funded ($3.8 million [R305A210269](https://ies.ed.gov/funding/grantsearch/details.asp?ID=4549)) [Diagnostic Assessment and Achievement of College Skills](https://daacs.net) (DAACS), which is a suite of technological and social supports designed to optimize student learning.
